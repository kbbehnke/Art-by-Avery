## About Art by Avery

This is a collection of 15 works my sister, Avery, has made throughout her college experience while working towards a Bachelor's in Fine Arts, with a focus on Graphic Design. She is graduating May 2026 and most of the works featured are from her BFA portfolio or BFA show she had in April 2026.

This collection features many different art forms including:
Graphic Design, Ceramics, Printmaking, and Watercolor


All watercolor pieces were done in preparation for her art show "Golden Days". These pieces are all based on memories or photos of Avery and I's childhood, mostly from the years 2009-2014. We are only one year apart, so these memories/pieces are very meaningful to me as well! I am very proud of her and I hope that you enjoy looking through her pieces!
